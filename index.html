Notable Work

Expert witnesses for big pharma

Royce consultants acted as experts for the University of Leeds, using state of the art electronic microscopes to track particles in an inhaler used to reduce lung disease.

Designing materials for safer nuclear energy

Royce researchers are working at the forefront of making nuclear energy cheaper, cleaner and safer, working with other industry experts to improve the performance of critical components in the fuel assemblies of reactors.

Battery efficiency for electric vehicles

Royce researchers, using Royce micorscopy, are helping make batteries lighter, cheaper, safer, and more efficient, critical for greater energy storage and to the further electrification of vehicles

Developing new electrotherapies to help treat brain tumours

QV Bioelectronics Ltd is developing a pioneering electrotherapy implant for the treatment of glioblastoma multiforme that aims to transform patient outcomes.
